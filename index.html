About Bike'N Dash

Bike'N Dash is a fast-paced Unity-based racing game, developed by LemmiDev PixelCraft. Control your bike through challenging tracks, avoid obstacles, and collect power-ups to become the fastest rider. My mission is to provide players with an entertaining and action-packed racing experience.

Support & Help
